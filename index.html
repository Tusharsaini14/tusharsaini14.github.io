"I've shipped ~100 production releases, integrated 20+ REST APIs and 10+ third-party SDKs, and built real-time communication systems now used by over 1.15 million people."

For 6 years I've specialized in the hardest problems in mobile: real-time communication systems at scale — live chat, one-to-one audio/video calling, and multi-user live streaming — all shipped, all in production, all battle-tested at 1M+ user scale.
